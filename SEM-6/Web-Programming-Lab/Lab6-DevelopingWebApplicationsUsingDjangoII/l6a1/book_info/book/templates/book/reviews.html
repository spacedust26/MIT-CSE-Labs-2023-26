Book Reviews

Review by The New York Times

Rating: ★★★★★

"The Hunger Games is a fast-paced, thrilling adventure with a gripping storyline that keeps you on the edge of your seat. Suzanne Collins expertly weaves together survival, politics, and deep moral questions about the human condition. A must-read for fans of dystopian fiction!"

Review by Kirkus Reviews

Rating: ★★★★☆

"A fascinating and brutal narrative that examines the impact of violence on society. Collins’ world-building is rich, and the characters, especially Katniss, are compelling. This novel will resonate with both young adults and older readers."

Review by Publishers Weekly

Rating: ★★★★★

"The Hunger Games offers a heart-pounding reading experience, combining action, suspense, and thought-provoking commentary on the nature of power and control. Katniss is an unforgettable protagonist who will stay with you long after the last page."

Review by School Library Journal

Rating: ★★★★☆

"A thrilling and brutal novel that explores the complexities of survival in a dystopian world. Collins’ portrayal of the Hunger Games is gripping and realistic, making for an unforgettable reading experience."
