About Scholastic Press

Scholastic Press is a major publisher of children's books, young adult literature, and educational materials. They are known for publishing popular titles such as *Harry Potter*, *The Hunger Games*, and *Clifford the Big Red Dog*. Scholastic's focus is on books that inspire, educate, and engage young readers, with a particular focus on fostering a love of reading.

Other Notable Publications

  • Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling
  • Clifford the Big Red Dog by Norman Bridwell
  • Percy Jackson Series by Rick Riordan
  • The Giver by Lois Lowry
